■ Overview

The Iron Throne
Awaits

Anuire is the political heart of Cerilia — a land of feudal kingdoms built upon the ruins of the greatest empire the world has ever known. When the last Emperor fell, the Iron Throne was left empty, and dozens of noble houses rose to claim it.

Now, princes and dukes wage a cold war of diplomacy, espionage, and open battle across the heartlands, each believing their bloodline grants them the divine right to rule all of Anuire.

Key Facts

Population ~Millions
Government Feudal Monarchy (fragmented)
Religion Haelyn (Noble War), Erik (Nature), Cuiraécen (Storms)
Landscape Fertile plains, ancient forests, fortified cities
■ Powers of the Land

Notable Domains

Avanil

The strongest claimant to the Iron Throne. Prince Avan commands the largest army in Anuire and pursues reunification through strength.

Boeruine

The Archduke of Boeruine challenges Avanil's claim with equal ambition and a fierce military tradition.

Roesone

A young barony carved from the wilds, Roesone represents the frontier spirit — self-made rulers building something new.

Medoere

A theocracy devoted to Ruornil, the god of magic. The only domain where the temple IS the state.

Elinie

A devout realm caught between powerful neighbors, maintaining independence through faith and determination.

Tuornen

A land of dark forests and ancient magic, Tuornen guards secrets older than the empire itself.

■ Defining Themes

Culture & Themes

01

The Game of Thrones

Political intrigue, succession crises, alliance and betrayal. Every court is a battlefield, every handshake a potential knife in the dark. Trust is the scarcest resource in Anuire.

Intrigue · Diplomacy · Betrayal
02

Legacy of Empire

The weight of history, ruins of grandeur, the dream of reunification. The Anuirean Empire once spanned the continent — its ghost haunts every throne room and battlefield.

History · Ruins · Reunification
03

Divine Right

Bloodlines as political currency, blood abilities as both gift and curse. The divine power flowing through royal veins is the ultimate claim to legitimacy — and the ultimate target.

Bloodlines · Power · Legitimacy
