█ 01
THE TRADING COAST

Brechtur hugs the northern coast of Cerilia — a land of fog-shrouded harbours, gothic trading cities, and merchant guilds that wield more power than any king. The Brecht people freed themselves from Anuirean rule generations ago and swore never to bow to a foreign power again. Instead, they built an empire of commerce.

In Brechtur, coin is the true measure of a person, and the great guilds operate as shadow governments, controlling trade routes, banking houses, and even private armies. But beneath the ledgers and trade agreements, darker forces move — smugglers, pirates, and those who trade in secrets rather than goods.

"In Brechtur, we do not kneel. We negotiate."

NOTABLE DOMAINS
Trade Capital

Müden

The greatest trading city on the coast. Its guild masters control more wealth than most kings and aren't shy about wielding it.

Mountain Realm

Danigau

A dark, mountainous realm where old traditions hold sway and the Countess rules with an iron will and ancient bloodline.

Cursed Land

Grabentod

The "Grave of God" — a cursed land where undead stir and the living must be ever vigilant against the darkness.

Naval Power

Rheulgard

A proud naval power that patrols the Krakennauricht strait. Its admirals are legends of the northern seas.

Free City

Treucht

A free city governed entirely by its merchant council — no kings, no lords, just profit and pragmatism.

CULTURE & THEMES

The Guild Wars

Economic warfare, trade monopolies, privateering as policy. In Brechtur, the battlefield is the marketplace, and the weapons are contracts, embargoes, and the occasional hired blade.

Trade Intrigue Power

Dark Waters

Pirates, smugglers, sea monsters, gothic horror at the docks. The seas around Brechtur teem with dangers both natural and supernatural, and every port has its shadows.

Horror Maritime Danger

Coin and Blood

Wealth as power, banking dynasties, the price of everything. In the merchant republics, bloodlines matter less than balance sheets, and every alliance has a price tag.
