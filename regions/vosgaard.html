█ 01
THE LAND OF BLOOD

Vosgaard is the most hostile region of Cerilia — a frozen wasteland of endless tundra, blood-soaked battlefields, and warlords who rule through fear and strength alone. The Vos people were the chosen of Azrai, the god of shadow, and his dark influence still courses through their blood. Here, might makes right in the most literal sense: chieftains earn their thrones by killing whoever sits upon them.

Diplomacy is for the weak. Alliance is temporary. And the only law that matters is the one enforced by axe and fire. Those who survive Vosgaard are forged into something harder than steel — but the cost of that forging is measured in blood.
