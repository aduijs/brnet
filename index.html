THE WORLD OF CERILIA

In a world where the blood of gods flows through mortal veins, heroes don't merely delve dungeons — they command armies, manage provinces, and shape the fate of nations through divine bloodright.

Created by TSR in 1995, Birthright remains the only D&D setting where political intrigue, domain management, and mass warfare sit at the heart of play — a Game of Thrones before Game of Thrones existed.

█ 02

THE SIX REGIONS

Each region of Cerilia is a world unto itself — with unique cultures, conflicts, and kingdoms to claim.

The Heartlands

ANUIRE

The shattered empire. Feudal lords battle for the Iron Throne while ancient bloodlines scheme in courts of steel and velvet.

The Wild North

RJURIK

Norse-inspired druids and jarls in untamed highlands where nature's law rules above all.

The Sunlit Lands

KHINASI

Desert cities of learning and trade, where merchants hold more power than kings.

The Trading Coast

BRECHTUR

Gothic harbours and mercantile guilds. Gold speaks louder than swords in these dark waters.

The Frozen Waste

VOSGAARD

Brutal warlords and blood-soaked tundra. Only the strong survive the endless winter.

█ Beyond the Veil

THE SHADOW
WORLD

A dark mirror of Cerilia where the dead do not rest and the Halfling realm bleeds into nightmare. Every step taken here costs a piece of your soul.
