██ Domain Overview

WHERE THE TEMPLE
IS THE STATE

Medoere is unique among Anuirean domains — it is a theocracy, a realm where the temple is the state. Ruled by the Archprelate of the Celestial Spell of Ruornil (the god of magic and the moon), Medoere is a small but spiritually powerful domain on the southern coast of Anuire.

The domain was once part of Diemed, but the faithful of Ruornil declared independence when the Baron of Diemed began persecuting followers of the moon god. Under the leadership of the Celestial Spell, Medoere established itself as an independent theocracy where the clergy govern all aspects of life.

Medoere is a realm of scholarship and mysticism. The temples of Ruornil serve as schools and libraries as well as places of worship. Magic is revered here, and wizards are welcomed rather than feared. The Archprelate governs through a council of high priests, making it one of the more democratic domains in Anuire — as long as you worship Ruornil.

“In Medoere, to question the temple is to question the state. To serve the god is to serve the realm.”

From the halls of the Celestial Spell

██ Political Landscape

CONFLICTS &
INTRIGUES

The Diemed Conflict

The Baron of Diemed has never accepted Medoere's independence. In his eyes, the theocracy is nothing more than a rebellious province — temple lands that rightfully belong under Diemed's crown. Border skirmishes are frequent, and Diemed's agents constantly seek to undermine Medoere's sovereignty through espionage, economic pressure, and occasionally open threats of invasion. The conflict defines Medoere's foreign policy and forces a domain of scholars and priests to maintain a standing military.

Faith and Magic

Ruornil is the god of magic and the moon, and his worship occupies a unique position in Cerilian theology. Unlike other faiths that view arcane magic with suspicion, the Celestial Spell actively encourages the study and practice of the magical arts. This makes Medoere a haven for wizards and scholars who face persecution elsewhere — but it also means the domain must navigate the deep distrust that many Anuireans hold toward unchecked magical power.

The Scholarly Tradition

Medoere's temples double as centers of learning. The Celestial Spell maintains extensive libraries and schools, making the theocracy a center of knowledge and education unmatched by domains many times its size. Scholars, astronomers, and students travel from across Anuire to study in Medoere's halls, giving this small domain an outsized influence in matters of lore, history, and arcane research.

██ Holdings

POWER STRUCTURE

Medoere's power base is overwhelmingly concentrated in its temple holdings. The Celestial Spell of Ruornil controls virtually all religious authority across the domain's three provinces. Military holdings are modest — sufficient for defense but not conquest. Law holdings are intertwined with the temple's authority, as religious law and civil law are one and the same in this theocracy.

◆ Temple Holdings — Dominant ◆ Law Holdings — Moderate ◆ Military — Modest ◆ Source Holdings — Contested

Note: The source holdings in Medoere's provinces are significant, given Ruornil's association with magic. Control of these sources is a constant source of tension with neighboring realms and independent wizards who covet the magical potential of Medoere's wild lands.
