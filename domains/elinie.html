■ Realm Overview

Faith Endures

Elinie is a devout realm caught in a dangerous geographic position — nestled between powerful neighbors and within striking distance of the Gorgon's Crown, the most feared domain in all Cerilia. The domain survives through faith, determination, and the fierce loyalty of its people.

The Countess of Elinie rules a realm where the temples of Haelyn (the god of noble war and justice) hold enormous influence. Religion permeates every aspect of Elinian life — from the laws that govern trade to the oaths that bind soldiers to service. This deep faith gives Elinie a moral authority that larger, wealthier domains cannot match.

Elinie's greatest challenge is simply survival. The domain lacks the military might of Avanil or Boeruine, and its eastern border faces constant pressure from the forces of darkness. Yet generation after generation, Elinie endures — a testament to what faith and courage can accomplish against overwhelming odds.

■ Political Landscape

Geopolitics of Faith

Between Giants

Elinie's survival is a matter of careful diplomacy and unwavering resolve. Sandwiched between larger, more powerful domains, the realm must navigate a treacherous political landscape where a single misstep could mean annexation — or worse. The Countess plays her allies close, maintaining temple alliances that provide spiritual legitimacy and military support when the banners are called.

The Faith Militant

Religion is not merely a comfort in Elinie — it is a weapon. The temples of Haelyn serve as both houses of worship and centers of military organization. Priests ride with soldiers, blessings are given before every battle, and the law of the land is inseparable from divine decree. This fusion of faith and arms makes Elinie's small forces punch far above their weight.

Eastern Shadows

The Gorgon's Crown casts a long shadow over Elinie's eastern frontier. Though the awnshegh himself rarely moves against the realm directly, his servants — twisted lieutenants and raiding parties of gnolls and worse — are a constant threat. Every generation of Elinians grows up knowing that the darkness is never far away, and that vigilance is the price of survival.

■ Domain Holdings

Power Structure

Temple Holdings

Strength Strong

The temples of Haelyn are the beating heart of Elinie. Their influence extends into every province, providing spiritual guidance, legal authority, and military chaplains. No decision of state is made without temple counsel.

Law Holdings

Strength Modest

The Countess maintains direct control over law in most provinces, though her reach is limited by the small size of the domain. Justice in Elinie is swift and deeply informed by Haelyn's doctrines.

Guild Holdings

Strength Modest

Trade in Elinie is modest compared to the mercantile powerhouses of western Anuire. The guilds that operate here do so under close temple oversight, ensuring commerce serves the common good.
