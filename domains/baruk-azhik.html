■ Overview

The Dwarven Kingdom

Baruk-Azhik is the greatest dwarven realm in Cerilia, carved into the mountains over millennia. The dwarves carved these halls long before humans came and intend to occupy them long after the last human kingdom falls.

The kingdom is a wonder of engineering. Great halls large enough for cities, lit by cunning reflections, heated by geothermal vents. The forges produce legendary weapons. The wealth in metals and gems is beyond reckoning.

Despite their power, the dwarves face persistent threats. Orogs, goblins, and the Gorgon's shadow. The greatest challenge may be internal: whether to maintain isolationism or engage with the human world.
