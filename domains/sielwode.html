■ Overview

The Elven Forest

The Sielwode is the largest elven domain in Anuire, a vast enchanted forest standing since long before humans came. Within its borders, the old world still lives — ancient magic, ageless trees, and beings who measure time in centuries.

The domain is ruled by the Sielwode Council, led by the Emerald Queen, a being of such ancient power that human records cannot trace her origins. She is fiercely protective and will use any means to defend her domain.

The forest itself is more than terrain — it is a living entity. Trees move to block paths, animals scout, and the air carries warnings. Armies find themselves lost, confused, and slowly picked apart.

■ Threats & Challenges

Political Situation

01

Human Encroachment

The greatest threat is the slow encroachment of human civilization. Logging, farming, and settlement eat at the borders. Each tree felled is felt as a wound.

Territorial · Environmental · Existential
02

Elven Isolation

Isolationist policy keeps the elves safe but disconnected. Some younger elves question whether complete isolation is sustainable.

Culture · Debate · Strategy
03

Ancient Grudges

The elves remember every injustice humans committed. These grudges color every interaction and make reconciliation extremely difficult.

History · Memory · Resentment
