THE SETTING

NOT JUST ANOTHER
D&D WORLD

In most D&D settings, you play a wandering adventurer. In Birthright, you play a ruler. Your character doesn't just have a sword and spells — they have a kingdom, an army, and a divine bloodline that grants supernatural power. Birthright is the only D&D setting where political intrigue, domain management, and mass warfare sit at the heart of play.

"Game of Thrones before Game of Thrones existed."

THE ORIGIN

IN THE BEGINNING, GODS WALKED AMONG MORTALS

Seven gods created the peoples of Cerilia and lived alongside them. But Azrai, the god of shadow, grew jealous and plotted to destroy his siblings and enslave all of creation.

THE CATACLYSM

THE BATTLE OF DEISMAAR

The gods made their last stand at Mount Deismaar. When they realized they could not defeat Azrai by force, they sacrificed themselves — all of them. The explosion of divine energy killed every god and scattered their essence across the battlefield. The mortals who survived absorbed this power into their blood.

THE POWER

DIVINE BLOOD IN MORTAL VEINS

The survivors of Deismaar became the first blooded scions — mortals carrying fragments of divine power. This blood passes through generations, granting supernatural abilities and, most importantly, the divine right to rule. Seven bloodlines, each tracing back to a fallen god, now course through the rulers of Cerilia.

THE GAMEPLAY

YOU DON'T JUST PLAY A CHARACTER YOU RULE A KINGDOM

Every season, you make domain actions: raise armies, build holdings, forge alliances, declare war, cast realm-level magic, or scheme in the shadows. Your decisions shape the political landscape of an entire continent. Birthright is part RPG, part grand strategy game — and nothing else in tabletop gaming comes close.

THE THREAT

BUT DARKNESS NEVER SLEEPS

The Awnsheglien — blooded scions twisted into monsters by Azrai's corruption — lurk at the edges of every kingdom. The Gorgon, the Spider, the Vampire... they were once human. They remember what they lost. And they want what you have: your blood, your throne, your soul.
