■ Overview

The True
Faith

The Orthodox Imperial Temple of Haelyn claims the distinction of being the oldest and most authentic expression of Haelyn's worship in all of Anuire. Founded in the Heartlands, the temple traces its lineage directly to the first priests who codified Haelyn's teachings after the Battle of Deismaar, and its leaders have always insisted that theirs is the only legitimate interpretation of the god's will.

Under High Priest Rhobher Nichaleir, the Orthodox Imperial Temple has adopted a conservative and traditionalist stance, resisting reforms and innovations that other temples of Haelyn have embraced. Nichaleir sees himself as the guardian of Haelyn's original doctrine — a doctrine that emphasizes duty, hierarchy, and the divine right of established authority.

The temple's position in the Heartlands gives it enormous strategic importance. It sits at the crossroads of Anuirean politics, with holdings in domains that border the territories of the Impregnable Heart to the south and the Northern Imperial Temple to the northwest. This central position makes it both a target and a power broker.

■ Persons of Interest

Key Personnel

Rhobher Nichaleir

High Priest

A rigid traditionalist who believes that Haelyn's original teachings have been corrupted by the other temples. Nichaleir is more scholar than warrior, preferring theological argument to military action, but he commands enormous respect among the faithful.

Keeper of Doctrine

Chief Theologian

Responsible for maintaining the purity of Haelynite teaching within the Orthodox tradition. This position carries great authority over what is considered acceptable belief and practice.

Legate of the Heartlands

Regional Commander

The senior priest responsible for day-to-day administration of temple holdings across the central Heartlands, managing hundreds of parish priests and minor temples.

■ Political Role

Guardian of
Tradition

The Orthodox Imperial Temple occupies a unique position in Anuirean politics as the self-proclaimed guardian of religious tradition. While it lacks the military might of the Impregnable Heart or the political connections of the Northern Imperial Temple, it commands respect through its claim to doctrinal authority and its position in the wealthy Heartlands.

The temple's relationship with secular power is complex. Nichaleir supports the idea of a restored empire but insists that any emperor must govern according to Haelyn's traditional teachings as interpreted by the Orthodox Temple. This makes the temple a potential kingmaker — or a stubborn obstacle — for any would-be emperor.

For player characters, the Orthodox Imperial Temple represents the conservative establishment. It can serve as a patron for traditional-minded regents, a rival for reformist temple holders, or a powerful faction that must be courted in any bid for the Iron Throne.
