■ Overview

The Impregnable
Heart

The Impregnable Heart of Haelyn is the largest and most powerful temple organization in all of Anuire. Founded in the centuries following the Battle of Deismaar, the temple has grown from a regional shrine into a continent-spanning institution that commands more wealth, influence, and political power than most landed domains. Its network of cathedrals, chapter houses, and fortified monasteries stretches across the entire Southern Coast and deep into the Heartlands.

The temple's name reflects both its spiritual mission and its temporal reality. The "Impregnable Heart" refers to Haelyn's teaching that true faith cannot be shaken — that the heart fortified by justice and honor is proof against all corruption. In practice, the temple's holdings are literal fortresses, designed to withstand siege and serve as rallying points in times of war. The temple maintains its own military orders, and its warrior-priests are among the most feared soldiers on the continent.

Under High Priest Hubaere Armiendin, the Impregnable Heart has pursued an aggressive policy of expansion, competing fiercely with the Orthodox Imperial Temple and the Western Imperial Temple for dominance over Haelyn's faithful. Armiendin believes that the temple's control over the Southern Coast gives it a divine mandate to lead all of Haelyn's worshippers, a claim that the other temples vehemently contest.

■ Sphere of Influence

Key Holdings

Diemed

The temple's strongest presence. The Impregnable Heart controls virtually all temple holdings in Diemed, making it the spiritual backbone of the domain. The High Cathedral in Aerele is the order's grandest edifice.

Medoere

A significant presence, though competing with the Celestial Spell of Ruornil. The Heart maintains cathedrals and chapter houses throughout the domain, ministering to those who follow Haelyn over the moon god.

Roesone

A growing presence in this young barony. The Heart sees Roesone as fertile ground for expansion, and has invested heavily in building temples and winning converts among the baroness's subjects.

Aerenwe

Moderate holdings in this forested domain. The temple competes with Nesirie's followers for the hearts of Aerenwe's people, who are famously independent-minded.

Ilien

The temple maintains a strong presence in this wealthy city-state, providing spiritual guidance to the merchant classes and competing for influence with the wizard-ruler el-Hadid.

Osoerde

Contested holdings in this troubled domain. The political instability of Osoerde has made it both dangerous and attractive — whoever controls the temples there will shape the domain's future.

■ Persons of Interest

Key Personnel

Hubaere Armiendin

High Priest

The leader of the Impregnable Heart and one of the most powerful religious figures in Anuire. Armiendin is a shrewd politician and a devout servant of Haelyn, who sees no contradiction between piety and ambition. He believes the temple's growth serves Haelyn's will.

Marshal of the Blessed Order

Military Commander

The commander of the temple's military orders, responsible for the defense of all fortified holdings and the training of warrior-priests. The Marshal answers only to the High Priest and commands thousands of armed faithful across the Southern Coast.

Chancellor of the Faithful

Chief Administrator

The administrative head of the temple's vast network of holdings, responsible for collecting tithes, managing estates, and coordinating the activities of hundreds of priests across multiple domains. A position of enormous practical power.

■ Political Role

Power Beyond
Borders

The Impregnable Heart of Haelyn is far more than a religious organization — it is a political power that shapes the fate of nations. With temple holdings in nearly every domain along the Southern Coast, the Heart can influence succession, rally popular support for or against rulers, and even field its own armies in times of crisis. No regent along the southern coastline can afford to ignore the High Priest's wishes.

The temple's rivalry with the Orthodox Imperial Temple and the Western Imperial Temple is the defining religious conflict of Anuirean politics. Each temple claims to be the truest expression of Haelyn's will, and the competition for temple holdings — the spiritual loyalty of provinces — is as fierce as any military campaign. The outcome of this three-way struggle will determine the shape of Anuirean religion for generations.

For player characters, the Impregnable Heart represents both an ally and a constraint. A regent who controls temple holdings must balance their own ambitions against the High Priest's demands. Conversely, a regent who earns the Heart's support gains access to healing magic, divine authority, and the political legitimacy that comes from Haelyn's blessing.
